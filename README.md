ShortLoop — YouTube Shorts Player
One file. Zero setup. Infinite scroll, your way.

🎬 Multi-Screen Playback
Watch 1 to 5 videos simultaneously — side by side in a sleek phone frame layout. Screens automatically resize and reflow as you add more. Perfect for monitoring multiple content creators or vibes at once.

🔀 Auto-Shuffle
Every session starts with your playlist in a fresh random order. No repeating patterns, no predictable sequences — pure variety every time.

⏱ Custom Random Delay
Set your own min and max seconds for how long each video plays before auto-advancing. Want a tight 3–7s burst? A relaxed 15–30s browse? You control the range — the app picks randomly within it each round.

📱 Realistic Phone Frames
Videos play inside beautifully rendered phone mockups complete with notch detail and gradient overlay. Looks great on any screen.

🔇 / 🔊 Mute Toggle
One-click mute and unmute with a clearly labeled button below the player. Starts muted by default (browser-safe), flip it on whenever you're ready.

⏯ Full Playback Controls
Pause / Resume — freeze auto-advance at any moment
Previous / Next — manually skip backward or forward
Visual progress bar — countdown showing exactly when the next video loads
⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
Control everything hands-free:
Space — pause/resume
← → — previous / next video
M — mute/unmute

📋 Playlist Management
Paste any YouTube URL — Shorts, regular videos, youtu.be links, embed URLs, raw video IDs — all handled automatically
Thumbnail previews for every video in your list
Now-playing highlight — always know which video(s) are active
Remove individual videos or clear the entire playlist in one click
Jump to any video directly from the list

💾 Persistent Settings
Everything saves automatically to your browser — your playlist, screen count, and delay range are all waiting for you on next open. No accounts, no cloud sync required.

🔒 Password Protection
Optionally gate the player behind a private password. Only people who know it can access your playlist. Session-aware — you only enter it once per browser session.

📦 Zero Dependencies
ShortLoop is a single standalone HTML file. No installation, no server, no internet connection required after first load. Download it, open it, done.
